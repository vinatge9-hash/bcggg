Our Story

Founded by the Moretti family in 1998, La Bella Cucina started as a tiny trattoria with one wood-fired oven and a simple goal: share the joy of honest Italian food. Over the years, we preserved family traditions while sourcing the best local ingredients to craft memorable meals.

We believe in slow cooking, welcoming service, and a menu that changes with the seasons. Whether it's our handmade pasta or our daily specials, every plate tells a story.
